## Apple Fritter Bread

![](https://bakewhatyoulove.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/apple-fritter-bread-finished-7h.jpg)

Based on the recipe from [Bake What You Love](https://bakewhatyoulove.com/apple-fritter-bread/).

#### For the apples

- 2 apples peeled, finely diced
- 1 Tbsp dark brown sugar packed
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp butter melted

#### for the cinnamon sugar

- 100 g brown sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon

#### for the batter

- 8 Tbsp butter, softened
- 99 g granulated sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tap vanilla extract
- 180 g AP flour
- 2 ½ tsp baking powder
- 76 g milk
- 76 g sour cream

#### Glaze

- 56 g powdered sugar
- 1 Tbsp milk
- ¼ tsp vanilla extract
- pinch of salt 

### Instructions 

- [ ] Combine the ingredients for the apple mixture in a bowl and mix.
- [ ] Combine the ingredients for the cinnamon sugar mixture in a small bowl and mix.
- [ ] Using a stand mixer with the paddle attachment, cream the butter and sugar.
- [ ] Add the eggs one at a time; mixing between each. Scrape the sides as needed. Add the vanilla and mix.
- [ ] Whisk the milk and sour cream together.
- [ ] Combine the flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl, then add it to the creamed butter and sugar. 
- [ ] Mix on low and slowly add the milk and sour cream mixture. Mix until just combined, scraping down the bowl as needed.
- [ ] Grease a loaf pan. Optionally, add a parchment paper sling.
- [ ] Transfer half the batter to the pan and smooth it out. Add half of the apple mixture and half of the cinnamon sugar mixture.
- [ ] Top with the remaining batter, the rest of the apples, and the remaining cinnamon sugar.
- [ ] Bake at 350˚F, until golden brown, about 50 minutes.
- [ ] Meanwhile, make the glaze by mixing the ingredients until smooth.
- [ ] Cool on a wire rack. -- Completely cool!
- [ ] Turn it out of the pan and drizzle with the glaze.
