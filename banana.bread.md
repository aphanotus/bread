## Banana Bread

### Ingredients

- 3 bananas (about 240g)
- 2 Tbsp butter, melted
- 1 Tbsp brown sugar
- 100 g honey
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 80 g AP flour
- 40 g WW flour (or oat flour)
- 1/2 cup chopped pecans

### Procedure

- [ ] Mash bananas.
- [ ] Add egg, vanilla, honey and sugar.
- [ ] Combine the dry ingredients.
- [ ] Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
- [ ] Add melted butter.
- [ ] Add the nuts.
- [ ] Pour into a greased loaf pan. (Optionally, line the pan with a  parchment paper sling.)
- [ ] Bake at 325ËšF for 45-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
- [ ] Cool on a wire rack.
